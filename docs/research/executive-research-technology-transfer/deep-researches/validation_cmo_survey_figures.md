**Research Date:** March 29, 2026  
**Purpose:** Validate specific figures from the CMO Club Russia × red_mad_robot GenAI adoption survey

|---------------|----------|--------|

---

## Survey Details

### Title
**"GenAI в маркетинге 2025: взгляд CMO"** (GenAI in Marketing 2025: CMO View)

### Conducted By
- **red_mad_robot** (Russian technology consulting company)
- **CMO Club Russia** (Association of Marketing Directors, supported by AKAR)

### Survey Period
May – July 2025

### Sample
- Respondents: Directors of Marketing (CMOs) of largest Russian companies
- Market coverage: Companies manage over 60% of marketing budgets of leading brands
- Industries: FMCG, retail, fintech, real estate development, and others

---

## Verified Figures

### 1. ChatGPT Usage: 91%
**Status:** ✅ VERIFIED

- **Source 1 (RB.RU, Dec 3, 2025):** "Главным рабочим ИИ-инструментом остаётся ChatGPT: им пользуются 91% CMO."
- **Source 2 (vc.ru, Dec 14, 2025):** "Используют: 91%"

**Additional context:**
- Satisfaction rate: 88%
- ChatGPT leads the second most popular tool by 32 percentage points

### 2. Midjourney Usage: 59%
**Status:** ✅ VERIFIED

- **Source (vc.ru, Dec 14, 2025):** "Используют: 59%"

**Additional context:**
- Satisfaction rate: 88%
- Second most popular GenAI tool among Russian CMOs

### 3. Overall GenAI Adoption: 93%
**Status:** ✅ VERIFIED

- **Source 1 (RB.RU, Dec 3, 2025):** "93% компаний применяют его в рабочих процессах"
- **Source 2 (CNews, Dec 1, 2025):** "93% российских СМО уже используют GenAI"

###工具使用排名 (Top Tools)
1. ChatGPT — 91%
2. Midjourney — 59%
3. DALL·E — 45%
4. Claude — 23%
5. Gemini — 23%
6. YandexGPT — 23%
7. Notion AI — 18%

### Impact Metrics
- 77% of CMOs report increased speed and quality of content
- 73% report accelerated work processes
- 50% report productivity increase without staff expansion
- 43% report reduced team workload

### Investment
- 64% allocate only 1-5% of marketing budget to GenAI initiatives
- Only 10% invest more than 11%

### Barriers
- 53% say content requires constant refinement
- 49% call it template-based
- 43% face hallucinations and errors
- 43% note data leak risks

### Areas with Low GenAI Adoption
- Event marketing: 64% do NOT use GenAI
- Brand management: 55% do NOT use GenAI
- Budget/ROI management: 57% do NOT use GenAI

---

## Conflicting Data

### No major conflicts found

The figures are consistent across multiple sources (RB.RU, CNews, vc.ru) which all cite the same original study.

### Minor Variations
- Some sources cite 93% of "teams" using GenAI vs. 93% of "CMOs" using GenAI — these appear interchangeable in context
- The 94% figure found in one RB.RU article (March 2026) appears to be from a different/subsequent survey

---

## International Comparison Notes

According to the research:
- Russian CMOs use GenAI daily (41%) — **2x the global average** (10-20%)
- Globally, 74-83% positive assessment (trend improving over 2 years)
- Russian CMOs already show confidence — GenAI is "no longer an option, but an integral part of the profession"

## Conclusion

**The figures 91% (ChatGPT) and 59% (Midjourney) are VERIFIED and accurate.**

Both figures come from the "GenAI в маркетинге 2025: взгляд CMO" study conducted by red_mad_robot and CMO Club Russia, published in late 2025. The data is consistent across multiple independent Russian business media outlets.
