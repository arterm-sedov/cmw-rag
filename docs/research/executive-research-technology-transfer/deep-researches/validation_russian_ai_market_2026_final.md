# Russian AI Market Validation: 2024-2026 Figures

**Search Date:** March 29, 2026  
**Purpose:** Validate key Russian AI market statistics for technology transfer analysis

---

## Executive Summary

The Russian AI market demonstrates robust growth of 25-30% annually, driven by domestic tech giants and increasing enterprise adoption. Market concentration remains extremely high, with five companies controlling approximately 95% of AI-related revenue.
