## 1. OpenAI Enterprise Report Findings (December 2025)

**Source:** OpenAI "The State of Enterprise AI 2025" (December 2025)  
**Methodology:** Aggregated telemetry from enterprise clients + survey of 9,000 employees in ~100 organizations

### 1.1 Key Growth Metrics

| Metric | Growth | Interpretation |
|--------|--------|----------------|
| Weekly ChatGPT Enterprise messages | **~8×** over year | Mass adoption across organizations |
| Average worker message volume | **+30%** | Increasing daily usage intensity |
| Structured scenarios (Projects, Custom GPTs) | **19×** from start of year | Shift from ad-hoc queries to repeatable processes |
| Reasoning tokens consumption per organization | **~320×** over 12 months | Massive shift toward complex inference modes |

### 1.2 Enterprise Adoption Patterns

- **Top vs median gap:** Workers at the 95th percentile send **6×** more messages than median employees
- **Leading organizations:** Have **2×** more messages per seat and deeper cross-team integration
- **Productivity self-reports:** 75% of respondents report improved speed or quality; 40-60 minutes time savings per day

### 1.3 Deployment Statistics

| Metric | Figure | Notes |
|--------|--------|-------|
| Organizations surveyed | ~100 | Enterprise clients |
| Employees surveyed | 9,000 | Anonymous, aggregated |
| New product capabilities | ~1 every 3 days | Fast innovation pace |
