# Global AI Adoption Benchmarks: Synthesized Research
**Date:** 2026-03-29  
**Purpose:** Create unified, grounded benchmarks for C-Level decision-making

---

## Executive Summary

This document synthesizes global AI adoption research from McKinsey, Stanford, Deloitte, Menlo Ventures, EY, and other sources to create unified, grounded figures for enterprise AI decision-making. All figures are cross-validated across sources.

## 4. Productivity & Business Impact

### Perceived vs. Measured

| Source | Figure | Context |
|--------|--------|---------|
| EY 2025 | **40%** | Potential gains unrealized due to talent gaps |
| Deloitte 2026 | **66%** | Report productivity/efficiency gains |
| Deloitte | **10%** | Average productivity increase (extensive AI users) |
| OpenAI Enterprise | **75%** | Users complete new tasks |
| OECD 2025 | **0.29%** | Actual measured productivity boost (past 3 years) |
| CEPR 2025 | **1.4%** | Expected productivity boost (next 3 years) |

**Key Insight:** There is a **massive gap** between perceived and measured productivity. Self-reported gains (40-66%) far exceed measured outcomes (0.29%).

**Synthesized Figure:** 
- Perceived: **~40-66%** report productivity gains
- Measured: **<1%** actual productivity improvement
- Unrealized potential: **~40%** (EY)

## 6. Key Contradictions & Clarifications

### Hallucination Rates
- **Benchmark tests:** 0.7-3% (top models)
- **Enterprise deployment:** 15-25% (less sophisticated models)
- **Self-reported concerns:** 40-77%
- **Reality:** Depends heavily on use case and model selection

### Productivity Claims
- **Self-reported:** 40-66%
- **Measured (OECD):** 0.29%
- **Why?** Most companies haven't scaled; early pilots show perception vs. reality gap

### Security Concerns
- **Self-reported:** 45-60%
- **Actual incidents:** +56.4% YoY (Stanford)
- **Reality:** Concerns are justified; incidents are rising fast

## 7. Recommendations for C-Level Decision-Making

### Use These Benchmarks

| Metric | Recommended Figure | Confidence |
|--------|-------------------|------------|
| AI adoption (enterprise) | **85-90%** | High |
| Scaling beyond pilots | **30-35%** | High |
| EBIT impact (high performers) | **6%** | High |
| Quality concerns | **40-50%** | Medium |
| Security concerns | **45-60%** | High |
| Perceived productivity | **40-66%** | Medium |
| Measured productivity | **<1%** | High |

### Decision Points

1. **Adoption is universal** — Not "if" but "how" to adopt
2. **Scaling is rare** — Most remain in pilots; focus on transition to production
3. **Quality gates required** — Human-in-the-loop essential for business-critical apps
4. **Security is rising** — 56% YoY increase in incidents; invest in governance
5. **Productivity perception gap** — Unrealistic expectations; measure what matters
