## Executive Summary

This plan combines:
1. **Business alignment** from original matrix (expertise transfer narrative, sales enablement)

The pack serves **two business goals**: (1) **expertise transfer** and (2) **selling AI knowledge** as an **enabler for C-Level executives** to ground their decisions.
