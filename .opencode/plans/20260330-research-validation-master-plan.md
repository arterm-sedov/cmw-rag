# Master Plan: Research Validation and Enhancement for Executive Research Pack

## Goal
Validate and enhance the research documents in `docs/research/executive-research-technology-transfer/report-pack/` to ensure they are:
1. Aligned to business purposes
2. Internally consistent and coherent
3. Grounded on real-world metrics
4. Non-contradicting
5. Properly structured with logical hierarchy
6. Focused on enabling executives to plan selling and transferring Comindware's AI expertise

## Business Purpose Alignment
The documents should enable executives to:
- Plan selling and transferring Comindware's AI expertise to clients
- Make informed decisions about AI implementation strategies
- Understand cost structures (CapEx/OpEx/TCO)
- Evaluate deployment options (cloud, on-prem, hybrid)
- Assess risks and compliance requirements
- Develop realistic ROI expectations

## Validation Approach

### Phase 1: Document Analysis and Gap Identification
- [ ] Analyze each target document for completeness, accuracy, and alignment with business goals
- [ ] Identify gaps in coverage, inconsistencies, and areas needing enhancement
- [ ] Check for duplicated content that should be consolidated
- [ ] Verify all claims are traceable to credible sources
- [ ] Ensure Russian language standards are followed (numbers, currency, typography)

### Phase 2: Deep Research on Specific Topics
- [ ] Competitor analysis: AI implementation approaches, pricing models, market positioning
- [ ] Pricing models: Validate Russian and international AI service pricing
- [ ] Market sizing: Validate Russian AI market figures and growth projections
- [ ] Technical validation: Verify AI infrastructure recommendations and observability practices
- [ ] Legal compliance: Validate 152-FZ and other Russian regulatory requirements

### Phase 3: Cross-Validation and Harmonization
- [ ] Cross-check figures, definitions, and recommendations across all documents
- [ ] Resolve contradictions or flag them explicitly with explanations
- [ ] Ensure consistent use of terminology, currency conversion rates, and metrics
- [ ] Verify that each topic is covered comprehensively in the appropriate document
- [ ] Check that scattered information is properly grouped

### Phase 4: Enhancement and Synthesis
- [ ] Add missing valuable information from deep research
- [ ] Group scattered information into coherent sections
- [ ] Clarify confused or conflicting content
- [ ] Generate original insights rather than copy-pasting
- [ ] Ensure perfect coherence: better, not bigger

### Phase 5: Final Review and Iteration
- [ ] Re-read all documents and cross-validate
- [ ] Verify all figures are sourced and validated
- [ ] Iterate until research is perfect and grounded in truth
- [ ] Ensure documents enable C-level decision making without teaching executives their job

## Verification Checkpoints

### Checkpoint 1: Source Validation (After Deep Research)
- All key figures validated with 2-3 independent sources
- Pricing data verified against official provider websites
- Market statistics cross-checked with reputable research firms
- Technical specifications validated against vendor documentation

### Checkpoint 2: Internal Consistency (After Synthesis)
- No contradictory statements between documents
- Consistent terminology and definitions throughout
- Unified currency conversion policy (1 USD = 85 RUB)
- Logical flow and section hierarchy in each document
- Proper cross-referencing between related documents

### Checkpoint 3: Business Alignment (Final Review)
- All content serves the purpose of enabling executive decision-making
- No internal repository paths in C-level summaries
- Focus on actionable insights rather than theoretical concepts
- Practical recommendations grounded in real-world constraints
- Appropriate depth for C-level audience (1-2 page summaries, detailed reports)
