## Editorial Standards Applied

### Language & Tone
- Authoritative, decisive voice
- Active constructions preferred
- Executive-level vocabulary
- No hedging or uncertainty phrases

### Structure
- Pyramid principle: conclusion first
- One idea per paragraph
- Short, front-loaded sentences
- No positional navigation references

### Content
- Zero editorial meta-text
- No self-referential document descriptions
- All claims traceable to sources
- No tautologies or pleonasms

### Consistency
- Unified terminology across all files
- Consistent numeric formatting
- Consistent cross-reference style
- Consistent heading structure
