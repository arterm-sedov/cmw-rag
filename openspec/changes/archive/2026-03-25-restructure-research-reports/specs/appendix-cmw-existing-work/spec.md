## Requirements
### Requirement: Describe existing CMW work as a transferable inventory
Appendix C SHALL describe the existing CMW work as an inventory of components, their roles, boundaries, and what exists today in a way that supports executive communication and transfer planning.

#### Scenario: Reader understands “what we have”
- **WHEN** a reader reviews Appendix C
- **THEN** they can identify which components exist, what each does, and how they fit together at a high level

### Requirement: Separate “what exists” from “how to implement”
Appendix C SHALL focus on describing existing work and SHALL NOT be the canonical source for phased implementation methodology.

#### Scenario: Implementation details are not duplicated
- **WHEN** a reader needs phased implementation guidance
- **THEN** Appendix C points to the methodology main report

### Requirement: Provide transfer-relevant artifacts list (non-quantitative)
Appendix C SHALL provide a transfer-relevant list of artifact types (code, configs, runbooks, evaluation assets) without duplicating economic tables.

#### Scenario: Transfer artifacts are enumerated without economics duplication
- **WHEN** transfer planning is discussed
- **THEN** Appendix C enumerates artifact categories and references Appendix B for acceptance/KT and the economics report for quantitative implications
